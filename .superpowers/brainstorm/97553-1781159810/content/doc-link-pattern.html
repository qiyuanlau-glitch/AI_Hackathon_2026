How should a cited guideline open the HOA document?

Your sketch: a "Guideline →" chip in the agent's reasoning links into the full HOA doc, scrolled to and highlighting the exact clause being cited. Three ways to reveal it. (Mockups are rough — judging the interaction, not the polish.)

A

Modal overlay (Recommended)

Click "Guideline →" → full-screen dialog opens with the HOA document, auto-scrolled to the cited clause, that clause highlighted. Close to return exactly where you were. Best for demo: big, focused, dramatic.

B

Slide-in side panel

Document slides in from the right, pipeline stays visible on the left. Good for comparing reasoning ↔ clause side by side, but cramped on a 640px-ish demo width.

C

Inline expand

The cited clause expands inline underneath the reasoning row (accordion). Simplest, no overlay — but you only ever see one clause, never the document as a whole.
